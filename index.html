About Him

Growing up in Pune, Adv. Sanjay M. Jain developed a passion for law & justice that led him to become an advocate in 1996. From that point, he dedicated himself to building a career rooted in trust, compassion, and a genuine love for helping others navigate their legal paths. With decades of experience, Adv. Jain is known for integrity, good communication, and trust. He specializes in agreement drafting, registration, property litigations, always with a mutually beneficial approach. Whether addressing cooperative housing issues or cheque dishonor cases, he is committed to clear and respectful client support.
